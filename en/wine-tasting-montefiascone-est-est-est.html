Food and wine

Wine Tasting in Montefiascone: Est! Est!! Est!!! and Local Cellars

Wine tasting in Montefiascone is closely tied to Est! Est!! Est!!!, the white DOC wine associated with the town and the volcanic landscape around Lake Bolsena. For visitors, the experience works best when wine, food and the old town are planned together.

Why taste wine here

Montefiascone is not just a label on a bottle. The town sits above Lake Bolsena, with vineyards shaped by volcanic soils and a wine story that is part of local identity. A tasting helps visitors connect the legend of Est! Est!! Est!!! with place, food and landscape.

How to approach a tasting

Do not assume that every cellar is open for walk-ins. Contact wineries or tasting venues directly, especially outside busy periods. Ask what is included, whether food pairings are available and whether the tasting is guided in English.

Pair wine with the town

A good wine day does not need to be complicated. Combine a tasting with a short walk in the historic centre, a viewpoint over Lake Bolsena and local food such as lake fish, handmade pasta or seasonal Tuscia products.

Frequently asked questions

Can I visit wineries without booking?

Sometimes it may be possible, but booking or checking directly is the safer approach because availability can vary.

Is Est! Est!! Est!!! only for aperitivo?

No. It can work with local food, especially lighter dishes, lake fish and vegetable-based plates.

Are prices listed here?

No. Prices can change, so check directly with the winery or venue before booking.
