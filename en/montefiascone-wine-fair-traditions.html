The fair and Est! Est!! Est!!!

The Wine Fair is the moment when Montefiascone most clearly presents the link between the town, its DOC wine and popular memory. The 2026 poster explicitly mentions Est! Est!! Est!!! and the story of the German baron Giovanni Defuk.

What is confirmed

The confirmed details are the dates, the 66th edition, the local wine focus and folklore and music events. The poster does not list times, wineries, tickets or a complete programme.

How to plan

During the fair the historic centre may be busier. Plan movement and return carefully, check parking and official details, and connect the visit with tastings and local food without leaving time-sensitive choices to the last minute.
