A late-summer lake event

Est-Lake Festival shifts the focus from the hill town to the shore of Lake Bolsena. It suits travellers looking for a more informal evening linked to water, music and summer social life.

What is confirmed

The selected dates, Lake Bolsena shore reference, street food, music, DJ sets and entertainment for children are confirmed. Times, precise areas and a full programme are not listed.

How to combine it with a stay

The festival pairs well with a lake day and a return to Montefiascone. Always check access, parking and operational updates, especially when travelling with children.
