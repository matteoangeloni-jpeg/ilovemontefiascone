Events calendar

Montefiascone Events Calendar

Montefiascone's events follow the rhythm of the old town, Lake Bolsena, wine culture and civic traditions. Use this page as a planning hub, then check official channels for operational updates.

Planning note

Recurring events can change by year. Dates, times, access and detailed programmes should be verified close to travel.

Main seasonal moments

Wine and local traditions

The Wine Fair and wine-related events are tied to Est! Est!! Est!!! di Montefiascone DOC, cellars, tastings and the agricultural memory of the town.

Summer culture

In summer, Montefiascone hosts cinema, music, festivals and public evenings that use squares, gardens and cultural spaces as open-air settings.

Religious and civic celebrations

Events such as the feast of Santa Margherita belong to the civic and devotional calendar of the town.

Choose the right event page

This calendar is an editorial starting point, not a replacement for official programmes. Open the dedicated page for each festival or celebration to separate confirmed dates and venues from background information. Events linked to wine, cinema, music, civic traditions and religious life serve different audiences, so the most useful choice depends on who is travelling and how much of the day is already planned.

Build the calendar into a stay

Use the event as the fixed part of the itinerary, then add Montefiascone's historic centre, the Rocca dei Papi or Lake Bolsena around it. Avoid relying on unconfirmed schedules, transport arrangements or ticket conditions. Before travelling, verify the latest programme with the organiser or official channels, especially when road access and public spaces may be affected.

How to use this calendar

Start with the event hub, then open the specific event page when it exists. For events without a confirmed programme, avoid relying on unofficial times or details.
