The patron saint and the town

Saint Margaret is one of Montefiascone's civic and religious reference points. The feast connects worship, local memory and the cathedral that defines the skyline.

What is confirmed

The poster confirms the celebration dates and the dedication to the patron saint. It does not list services, times, processions or side events in detail.

For visitors

This is a useful moment to read Montefiascone as a living community, not only as a viewpoint. Before visiting, check current religious, civic and traffic updates.
