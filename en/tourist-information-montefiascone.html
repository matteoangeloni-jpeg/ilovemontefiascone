Montefiascone tourist information

A reference page for planning a visit to Montefiascone: how to reach the tourist office, how to find your way around a hilltop town, and which practical guides to read before you travel to this part of Tuscia.

Tourist office and contacts

The institutional point of reference is the Tourist Office at Largo del Plebiscito, 1, Montefiascone (VT). For enquiries you can use the phone (+39 0761 832060) and email (info@comune.montefiascone.vt.it). For current opening hours and services, contact the office or the official municipal channels.

Getting around town

Montefiascone is explored mostly on foot. The historic centre is on a hill and partly restricted to traffic, so it helps to park lower and walk up (see parking). To plan your arrival, see how to get here.

Before you visit

Three useful checks: opening hours of churches and sites (which vary by season), any events that affect traffic and crowds, and the best season for your interests (see the best time to visit). For official updates, refer to the municipal channels.

Frequently asked questions

Where is the tourist office in Montefiascone?

At Largo del Plebiscito, 1, Montefiascone (VT). For opening hours and services, contact the office or the official municipal channels.

How do you get around Montefiascone?

Mostly on foot. The centre is hilly and partly traffic-restricted, so park lower and walk up. A town map helps with the climbs and main points.

What should you check before visiting?

Opening hours of sites, any events affecting traffic, and the season that best suits your interests. Verify details through official channels.
