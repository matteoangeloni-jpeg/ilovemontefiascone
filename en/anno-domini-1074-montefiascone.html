Montefiascone returns to 1074

A.D. 1074 uses the streets of Montefiascone as a living medieval setting. For international visitors, it is an accessible way to read the town through history, costume, food and public performance.

The event is suitable for families, history lovers and travellers who want to experience Montefiascone beyond monuments and viewpoints.

What to see

Historical reenactment and procession

The central theme is the historical reenactment, with a procession, medieval costumes and scenic settings. The event material refers to the meeting between Matilde di Canossa and Pope Gregory VII, a concise historical frame for the 11th-century atmosphere.

Camps, falconry and street performers

The organizer's communication includes camps, falconry, music, performances, street artists, games and medieval markets. It also refers to more than 100 reenactors involved in the staging.

Rocca dei Papi and the old town

Rocca dei Papi fortress and the Giardini del Palazzo Vescovile are the most evocative places for the festival. The old town connects them through alleys, squares and walkable routes.

Food and wine

The Locanda del Pellegrino gives the festival a strong food-and-wine dimension. The available material mentions tortucce, Est! Est!! Est!!! wine, arrosticini and mead.

Affiliated restaurants named in the event material include Casti, Enosteria Antichi Sapori, Mammapappa and La Fraschetta. Check current arrangements directly before making plans.
