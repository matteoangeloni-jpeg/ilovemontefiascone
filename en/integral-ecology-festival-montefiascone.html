2026 information

  • Official dates: 2, 3, 4 and 5 July 2026.
  • Public sources used: Montefiascone Summer 2026 poster and the official festival website.
  • The official website confirms the theme Beyond the Deserts, the opening on 2 July 2026 at 18:00 in Piazza Santa Lucia Filippini and Mogol as the opening guest.
  • The organiser publicly associated with the event is Associazione Rocca dei Papi per un'Ecologia Integrale ETS.

What integral ecology means here

The official description presents integral ecology as an approach linking environmental, territorial, economic and social sustainability. For visitors, the festival adds a cultural layer to the summer calendar.

What is confirmed

The edition, dates and overall theme are confirmed by both the municipal poster and the event site. The official festival website adds a stronger layer of certainty on the opening slot: Thursday 2 July at 18:00 in Piazza Santa Lucia Filippini, with Mogol announced for the opening event.

What still requires caution is the full schedule of talks, guests and session-by-session timing. This page intentionally keeps only the framework that is already public and verifiable.
