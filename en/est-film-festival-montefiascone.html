Cinema in the summer calendar

Est Film Festival gives Montefiascone's summer calendar a clear cinema identity. The official source refers to Italian feature films by new authors, first or second works.

Meetings, competition and the wider format

The official festival website gives Montefiascone a clearer frame than the municipal poster alone: the town hosts the twentieth local edition within a broader Lago di Bolsena network that unfolds across different places and moments of the year.

What remains safe to state on this page is the structure of the event: Italian feature films by emerging directors, screenings connected with conversations, and a cultural evening programme worth pairing with an overnight stay. Film titles, guest lists and exact screening times should still be checked on the official programme.

The most practical published detail so far is the venue reference to Piazzale Frigo in the historic centre, listed by the festival as a base for evening screenings and meetings. For travellers, that immediately clarifies how cinema can fit into an old-town evening rather than a daytrip-only visit.

How to use it in a trip

The festival can be a reason to stay several evenings in town, combining cinema, old-town walks and Lake Bolsena visits during the day.
