Frequently asked questions

What is the source for the 2026 summer calendar?

The main source is the Summer 2026 poster published by the Municipality of Montefiascone on 10 May 2026, together with the official channels of each event where available.

Are times and ticket details included here?

This page summarises the main confirmed dates. Times, access details and operational information may change, so check official channels before travelling.

Why should some events still be rechecked shortly before travelling?

Because municipal posters, organiser channels and later updates do not always move at the same speed. The dedicated event page is the right place for the last verification step.
