Frequently asked questions

What is the source for the 2026 summer calendar?

The source is the Summer 2026 poster published by the Municipality of Montefiascone on 10 May 2026.

Are times and prices listed here?

No. This guide reports dates and descriptions confirmed by the poster, without inventing times, prices or unpublished details.

Which event does not have a dedicated page?

The children's historical parade with Republic Day is included in the hub calendar because it has already taken place at the time of this guide.
