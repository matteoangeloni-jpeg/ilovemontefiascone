ATB Festival 2026 Line-up and Schedule

ATB Festival hosts a diverse artistic line-up ranging from rap and electronics to indie and acoustic singer-songwriter performances at the Rocca dei Papi in Montefiascone:

Thursday 9 July 2026

Lorenzo Cantarini Live

📍 Main Stage – Rocca dei Papi | 🕦 10:30 PM

Enveloping melodies, guitars blending with electronics, and a rock attitude. A show capable of shifting from intimate moments to powerful energy.

Friday 10 July 2026

Loner Tennis Club (Live Electronics)

📍 Main Stage – Rocca dei Papi | 🎫 Free Entry

An elegant and unpredictable electronic journey featuring synth-pop, enveloping grooves, and club culture under the Tuscia summer sky.

ELASI DJ Set Live

📍 Main Stage – Rocca dei Papi | 🕦 12:00 AM (Midnight)

Electronic pop, global contaminations, and refined rhythms. A colorful DJ set full of surprises, perfect for a summer night near the lake.

Saturday 11 July 2026

Giovanni Ti Amo Live

📍 Acoustic Stage | 🕦 10:00 PM

Guitar, vocals, and songs that feel like unsent letters. Direct and soft emotions under the lights of a summer sunset.

Ketama126 Live

📍 Main Stage – Rocca dei Papi | 🕦 12:00 AM (Midnight)

One of the most prominent voices in the new Italian scene and a member of Lovegang126. A unique identity fusing rap, punk, songwriting, and electronics.

Sunday 12 July 2026

Karakaz Live

📍 Main Stage – Rocca dei Papi | 🕦 11:30 PM

Electronics, experimentation, and visceral energy that redefines the sound of the Rocca. An immersive performance full of aesthetic power.

Solidarity: AVIS Montefiascone Blood Donation on 11 July

Donate blood on 11 July with AVIS Montefiascone. As a thank-you gesture, every donor will receive a free ATB Festival 2026 beer ticket. A special initiative dedicated to those who turn solidarity into action.

A young event in the old town

ATB Festival combines music, social atmosphere and craft beer. Its old-town setting makes it more than a concert: the historic centre becomes part of the evening.

Good to know before you go

The poster confirms the dates, the music focus and the craft beer angle. Concert times, line-up, exhibitors and access rules aren't listed yet: check the festival's official channels before you travel.

Practical caution

Check updates before travelling, especially if arriving by car. On busy evenings, peripheral parking and a final walk into the centre may be the most realistic approach, following local signs.
