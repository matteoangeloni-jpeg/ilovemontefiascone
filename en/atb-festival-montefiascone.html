ATB Festival 2026 in Montefiascone

Four summer days of music, craft beer, food and glitch-inspired festival energy in Montefiascone.

From 9 to 12 July 2026, Montefiascone picks up the ATB Festival signal again: four days built around music, craft beer, food and a collective summer atmosphere, with a visual identity inspired by glitches, intercepted transmissions and "future detected" energy.

The 2026 teaser confirms Montefiascone and Loc. Porticella, with free entry. While the detailed programme is still to be announced, this page collects the confirmed information and useful links for planning a visit during Montefiascone's summer events season.

What to expect

Music and atmosphere

Four evenings built around music and the simple pleasure of being together: ATB turns a space in Montefiascone into a meeting point, where the crowd is as much a part of the night as the stage.

Craft beer and food

Craft beer is one of the festival's signature ingredients, to enjoy alongside the food on offer and to slow down between one moment and the next.

Sci-fi and glitch identity

The 2026 visual identity plays with glitches, intercepted signals and a sci-fi imagery of anomalies and "future detected" — a language that gives the festival an instantly recognisable character.

Montefiascone as a summer setting

The hilltop town above Lake Bolsena is the backdrop: around the evenings there are views, wine and corners of the old town still to discover.

Why go

  • It is one of Montefiascone's summer 2026 events.
  • A good fit for visitors interested in music, beer and food.
  • It has a distinctive, contemporary visual identity.
  • It can be combined with Lake Bolsena, wine and a weekend in Tuscia.
