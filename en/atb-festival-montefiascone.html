A young event in the old town

ATB Festival combines music, social atmosphere and craft beer. Its old-town setting makes it more than a concert: the historic centre becomes part of the evening.

Good to know before you go

The poster confirms the dates, the music focus and the craft beer angle. Concert times, line-up, exhibitors and access rules aren't listed yet: check the festival's official channels before you travel.

Practical caution

Check updates before travelling, especially if arriving by car. On busy evenings, peripheral parking and a final walk into the centre may be the most realistic approach, following local signs.
