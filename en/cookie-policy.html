Cookie policy

This page lists the cookies used by the site, their purpose and duration, and explains how to accept, decline or change your choice at any time.

Necessary technical cookies

This site does not set profiling or advertising cookies. For technical operation it only uses components that do not require consent because they are essential to the service:

  • a service worker for offline caching (if supported by the browser);
  • sessionStorage to temporarily store the homepage weather data (duration: browsing session);
  • localStorage to remember your cookie-banner choice (duration: until manually changed or browser data is cleared).

Analytics cookies — Google Analytics 4 (only with consent)

We use Google Analytics 4 for anonymous, aggregated visit statistics. These cookies are set only after you click "Accept": before consent, no Google script is loaded and no analytics cookie is created (Google Consent Mode v2, defaults set to "denied").

CookiePurposeTypical duration
_gaDistinguishes users anonymously for aggregated statistics.2 years

For the legal basis and data transfer to Google, see the privacy policy.

How to change or withdraw your choice

You can change your mind at any time: use the buttons at the top of this page, or the "Cookie preferences" link in the footer of every page. Clicking "Reset cookie preferences" clears your saved choice and reloads the page, showing the banner again so Google Analytics stops receiving new data until you accept again.

Third-party services

Some resources come from external services that may set their own technical cookies or receive connection data, including your IP address:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com): typefaces, on all pages.
  • Open-Meteo (api.open-meteo.com): weather data on the homepage.
  • Maps (Map page only): Leaflet from unpkg.com and tiles from OpenStreetMap, OpenTopoMap and ArcGIS.

Please refer to those services' own policies for details.
