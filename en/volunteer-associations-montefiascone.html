Volunteer associations in Montefiascone

Discover the civic and social network of the Faliscan village: healthcare, blood drives, emergency response, social care, and cultural heritage.

Overview

Sectors
Healthcare, Security, Culture, Social Care
Main Entities
AVIS, AIDO, ASVOM, Solidarietà Falisca, Amici del Colle

AVIS Comunale Montefiascone OdV

AVIS Comunale Montefiascone OdV is the local branch dedicated to promoting and organizing voluntary blood and plasma donations throughout the municipal area.

The association schedules regular donation drives via a dedicated mobile blood unit stationed in Piazza Vittorio Emanuele, managing educational campaigns in schools.

AVIS actively promotes a culture of solidarity and healthcare prevention, acting as a central pillar for community public health and active citizenship.

AIDO Montefiascone - "O. Cacalloro" Group

The AIDO Montefiascone group, named in memory of Ornella Cacalloro, raises awareness in the local community on the ethical value of post-mortem organ, tissue, and cell donations.

Among the group's historic events is the spectacular descent of the AIDO Befana from the Clock Tower and the inclusive walk "Lake Without Barriers".

AIDO disseminates clear, scientifically correct information to encourage informed consent on biological donations, blending serious content with popular local festivities.

AS.VO.M. - Civil Protection Montefiascone

AS.VO.M. (Montefiascone Volunteers Association) is the local civil protection organization active since 1994, safeguarding the community and managing logistics.

AS.VO.M. manages an operations room active 24/7 situated at Piazzale della Stazione in Montefiascone, coordinating emergency tasks and territorial monitoring.

AS.VO.M. volunteers respond to extreme weather events, forest fires, natural disasters, and manage security during major municipal public events.

Associazione Solidarietà Falisca

The Associazione Solidarietà Falisca, serving the area since 1991, provides social transport and daily assistance to patients, disabled individuals, and fragile elders.

The organization operates on a 24/7 basis, deploying equipped ambulances and specialized vans designed for secure wheelchair transport.

Solidarietà Falisca actively reduces the social isolation of vulnerable groups, facilitating their access to medical appointments, rehabilitation therapies, and social services.

Associazione Culturale Amici del Colle

The Associazione Culturale Amici del Colle is dedicated to preserving, safeguarding, and re-enacting the historical, religious, and popular traditions of Montefiascone.

The association organizes the acclaimed Living Nativity inside the gardens of the Bishop's Palace, and curates historical pageants highlighting ancient crafts and farming life.

Amici del Colle promotes the cultural tourism and historical memory of the town, engaging local citizens in massive community performances and educational displays.

How to Support

  • Donate via the Italian 5x1000 tax allocation scheme.
  • Attend solidarity and cultural events in the town's calendar.
  • Offer your time by signing up as a local volunteer.
  • Make direct donations to support the purchase of emergency gear.
