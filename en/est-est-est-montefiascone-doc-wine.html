Est! Est!! Est!!! di Montefiascone DOC

Est! Est!! Est!!! is the white wine most closely linked to Montefiascone. Its name is unusual, its legend is medieval, and its identity belongs to the volcanic hills around Lake Bolsena.

At a glance

Style
Historic white DOC wine
Place
Montefiascone and Lake Bolsena
Best with
Lake fish, light dishes, tastings

The legend behind the name

The famous story tells of a traveller whose servant marked good inns with the Latin word Est, meaning "it is here". In Montefiascone, the wine was considered so good that the mark became Est! Est!! Est!!!. The legend is also connected with San Flaviano.

Grapes and taste profile

The wine is generally associated with local white grape traditions such as Trebbiano, Malvasia and Rossetto, depending on the producer and the official rules in force. In the glass, expect a fresh, accessible white wine rather than a heavy or overly aromatic style.

How to taste it in Montefiascone

Use it as a thread through the visit: a glass after the old town, a tasting with local food, or a cellar visit if available. Availability, opening days and tasting formats vary, so contact producers or local operators before planning around a specific winery.

Frequently asked questions

What is Est! Est!! Est!!!?

Est! Est!! Est!!! is the historic white DOC wine associated with Montefiascone, produced in the volcanic landscape around Lake Bolsena.

Why does the wine have this name?

The name comes from a medieval legend in which a traveller's servant marked good inns with the word Est; in Montefiascone the wine was so appreciated that the sign was repeated three times.

What does it pair with?

It works well with lake fish, simple pasta dishes, light starters and relaxed tastings, depending on the producer and vintage.
