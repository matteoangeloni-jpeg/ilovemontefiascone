Living Heritage

A heritage suspended between history and volcano

Montefiascone rises on a volcanic tufo hill overlooking the Lake Bolsena caldera. This unique landscape shaped its history, from the medieval fortress of Rocca dei Papi to the grand dome of the Cathedral of Santa Margherita.

Today, the town combines the heritage of the Via Francigena pilgrim path with active local traditions, like the legendary Est! Est!! Est!!! wine and a passion for historic motorsport.
