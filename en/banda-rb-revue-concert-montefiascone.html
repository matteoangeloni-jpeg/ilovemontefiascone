What is confirmed

The internal source confirms the performer, date, time and place: La Banda R&B Revue, Friday 19 June 2026, 21:00, Piazzale Roma in Montefiascone.

Why it matters for visitors

The concert adds a live-music stop to a summer evening in the historic centre. Visitors can combine the event with a walk through the upper town or an early evening view towards Lake Bolsena.

How to fit the concert into an evening

Piazzale Roma is close to Montefiascone's historic centre, so visitors can combine the live performance with an early walk through the town rather than treating it as an isolated stop. The event belongs to the 2026 summer programme and offers a contemporary counterpoint to the town's churches, papal history and wine culture. Leave enough time to reach the venue without rushing through the steep central streets.
