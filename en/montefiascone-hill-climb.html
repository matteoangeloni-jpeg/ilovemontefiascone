The official 2026 identity of the event

The already published URL stays unchanged, but the public 2026 material points clearly to the official event name: 29th Lago-Montefiascone. Public press material also reconnects the race with its long local tradition and with the role of Montefiascone as a hill town above Lake Bolsena.

For international visitors, the key point is not the sporting regulation itself but the context: this is a historic hill climb with a recognised ACI framework, local institutional backing and strong visibility for the territory.

Route and safety

The confirmed technical detail is the S.P. Lago di Bolsena section from km 5+150 to km 0+400. For visitors and photographers, the safest rule is caution: spectator areas, road closures, parking and access instructions may be defined close to the event.

How to read it as a visitor

The climb links the lake landscape with the hill town. Use this guide for context, not as a substitute for regulations, operational notices or safety instructions on the event days.
