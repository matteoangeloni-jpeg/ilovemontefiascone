Choose events by travel interest

Montefiascone's calendar brings together wine traditions, film, live music, religious celebrations and community initiatives. Culture-focused travellers can start with Est Film Festival and heritage events, while food and wine visitors may prefer the Wine Fair and pages connected with Est! Est!! Est!!!. Families and slow travellers should look at venue, duration and access before building an event into the day.

Events within a Lake Bolsena stay

Montefiascone can serve as an evening base after visiting Lake Bolsena or nearby towns in the Tuscia area of Lazio. Keep the event itself as the fixed point, then add only one or two daytime stops. This avoids overloading the itinerary and leaves room for traffic, parking restrictions or weather. Each dedicated event page distinguishes confirmed information from details that still require an official update.

How to plan responsibly

Use this hub for orientation, then verify access rules, traffic changes, detailed programmes and any last-minute updates through official event channels before travelling.
