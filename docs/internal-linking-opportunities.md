## Link Risks to Avoid

- Do not over-link from broad hubs to too many near-duplicate pages.
- Do not make every itinerary link back to every event.
- Do not use the same anchor text for distinct pages that answer different intents.
- Do not link into FR or technical-only pages from the public perimeter.

## Minimal Safe Linking Pattern

Each hub should link to no more than:

- 5 core spokes
- 3 utility pages
- 2 nearby clusters

That keeps crawl paths clear while preserving topical focus.
